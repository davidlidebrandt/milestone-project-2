How To Play

The goal of the game is to find pairs of cards by clicking on them. Click on a card to turn it over and revele it and then click on another one to try to find a pair. If a pair is found the cards will remain shown, if no pair is found the cards will turn back over to their backside and you can try again.

Keep in mind that their is a limited amount of time to find the next pair of cards. If the time of the timer runs out the game is lost. If all pairs of cards are found the game is won and the final score will be shown.

You can always return home or restart the game by using the links/buttons in the top left menu on the game page.
